Biography

I'm an Algorithm Researcher of the Computer Vision Group, 4Paradigm.
Before my industry career, I received my B.Sc. degree in Computer Science and Technology in June 2016 from Nanjing University.
I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Dec 2021, under the supervision of Prof Jianxin Wu, and I was in the LAMDA Group.

From Dec. 2019 to Dec. 2020, I was visiting Dr. Yin Li's group under the grant of China Scholarship Council (CSC).

We are hiring highly motivated interns/full-time algorithm engineers, If you have interests in my research topic, please contact me for more details!

Research Interests

My research interests include: Computer Vision and Machine Learning. Currently, I'm focused on:

  • Video Understanding.

  • Resource Constrained Deep Learning in computer vision tasks.

  • Real-World, Low Cost Data Generation.

  • Video related vision tasks.
